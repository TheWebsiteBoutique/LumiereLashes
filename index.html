Good to Know

Studio Policies

Please take a moment to read through our policies — they help me provide the best experience for every client.

01
Cancellations & Rescheduling

I require at least 48 hours' notice for cancellations or reschedules. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice will incur a 50% service fee. No-shows are charged 100% of the scheduled service.

02
Late Arrivals

Please arrive on time or up to 5 minutes early. Arriving more than 10 minutes late may result in a modified service or rescheduling, as late arrivals impact subsequent clients.

03
Fill Policy

To qualify for a fill appointment, you must retain a minimum of 40% of your previous set. Sets below this threshold will be priced as a new full set. Fills are available up to 3 weeks post-application.

04
Health & Contraindications

Please arrive with clean, makeup-free lashes. Notify me in advance if you have known allergies to adhesives, eye conditions, or are currently on Accutane, which affects lash retention.

05
Patch Test Policy

All first-time clients are encouraged to request a complimentary patch test 48 hours before their appointment. This is required if you have a history of skin sensitivities or allergies.

06
Satisfaction Guarantee

Your happiness matters. If you have any concerns about your lashes, please reach out within 72 hours of your appointment and I'll arrange a complimentary adjustment at no charge.
